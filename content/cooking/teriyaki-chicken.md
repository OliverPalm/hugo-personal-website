- Prep time: 10 min
- Cook time: 20-30 min
- Servings: 4

## Ingredients
- 750 g Chicken
- 4 Tbsp soy sauce
- 4 Tbsp mirin 
- 4 Tbsp sake

## Directions
1. Mix all the ingredients for the sauce
2. Chop the chicken into bite sized pieces 
3. Fry the chicken until cooked through
4. Add the sauce to the chicken and cook until reduced
